About Us

Alwaarith Nursery and Primary School was established in September 2006 to provide both quantitative and qualitative education to the growing Muslim population in Lagos State. Located at 7, Ajolojuota Street, Oke Abiye, Alagbado, the school is dedicated to nurturing well-rounded Muslim personalities who are not only academically proficient but also spiritually, religiously, and morally conscious in any environment.

Alwaarith began in 2006 with only Kindergarten, Nursery, and Primary One classes. Over time, it expanded to offer a complete range of classes from Kindergarten One to Primary Six. Our teachers are compassionate and dedicated, excelling both academically and morally. The school is renowned for its high academic standards and religious integrity. Alwaarith's outstanding performance in external academic quizzes and debates has made it a preferred choice for parents in and around Lagos.

In 2012, just seven years after the establishment of the Nursery and Primary School, Alwaarith Model College (AMC) was founded to ensure a seamless transition for our pupils into secondary education. AMC began with only JSS 1, starting with seven students who later progressed to JSS 2 and JSS 3. AMC has fully integrated Edu-Tech into our curriculum, from teaching coding and programming to facilitating the transition of our SS3 students to a branch of the school named Infolab Institute of Technology.
